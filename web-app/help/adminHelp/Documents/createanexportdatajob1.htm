User's Guide  Exporting Dataset Explorer Findings

Create an Export Data Job

To export Dataset Explorer findings to your local machine:

1.  Click the tranSMART Dataset Explorer tab to display the Dataset Explorer window.

2.  In the left pane of the Dataset Explorer window, click the appropriate tab: Navigate by Study or Across Trial.

3.  Select the study for which you want to export data.

4.  Define the cohorts whose data points are of interest.

If you do not enter any data in the subsets, you see the following message:

Click OK, then click the Comparison tab. Load data into at least one subset to continue.

5.  Click the Data Export tab:

The Data Export page appears with your selected cohorts:

6.  Select the check boxes to indicate the data types and file formats you want to use for export. You can select multiple format types at one time.

7.  Decide whether you want to export all the data associated with your cohort or filter it by specifying individual concepts to export.

When you filter the data, only the concepts you indicate will be included in the exported data. Because some studies have hundreds of concepts associated with each patient, adding a filter(s) allows you to limit/pinpoint the data exported to only what you need to work with.

□    To export all the data associated with the cohort, go to step 10.

□    To filter the data to only export a selected set of concepts, continue with
step 8.

8.  Click the Advanced Options button in the navigation bar below the Instructions:

9.  Drag concepts from the list of studies into the box at the bottom of the Data Export page:

Remember that only the concepts included in this box will be exported for the selected subset.

10. Click Export Data at the bottom of the tranSMART browser window. The command starts a job and a Job Status window displays the job’s progress:

□    Run in Background button - The job could take several minutes or hours depending on the amount of data selected. Click this button to run the job in the background, which allows you to continue with other analyses and cohort selection while the job completes.  You may log out of tranSMART at this point and log in later to retrieve your export job.

□    Cancel Job button – Click this button to cancel the job currently running. This job will display in the Export Jobs list (see step 2 in Download an Exported Subset) with a status of Cancelled.

11. Click the Export Jobs tab to access completed jobs or to check the status of a pending job.
